FAQ

Quick answers before you message us

Everything is handled through chat so we can confirm the exact item, fees, and shipment details with you.

How do I place an order?

Watch our live shopping sessions, comment CF, or send a screenshot through Facebook Messenger. We’ll confirm availability and invoice you in chat.

When do I pay shipping?

International shipping is paid after your items are packed and the final shipping quote has been confirmed.

Do you ship worldwide?

Yes. We arrange worldwide shipping, including the USA, UAE, Philippines, Singapore, Canada, Australia, and more.

Can I combine multiple orders?

Yes. Most international customers keep an open box so multiple orders can ship together later.

How long can you hold my items?

Paid items may be stored free of charge for up to 30 days. Please message us for longer storage requests.

Can you help me find an item?

Yes. Send a photo, product name, or brand details and we’ll do our best to find it in Thailand.
