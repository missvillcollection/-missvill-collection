Important Notice

Final shipping fee will be confirmed after packing. Shipping is calculated by actual weight or volumetric weight, whichever is greater.

Formula:

Width × Length × Height (cm) ÷ 5000

Destination tax / import tax is not included.

Estimated delivery time: 7–14 business days.
